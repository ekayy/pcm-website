The Story Behind PCM

PCM is a premier emergency restoration company in the Southern California area. We presently provide services in Los Angeles, Ventura, San Bernardino, Riverside and Orange Counties. We specialize in commercial and residential water damage, fire damage, and mold remediation services. Our company is fully licensed, bonded and insured. Our technicians are fully certified and trained.

Whether you have experienced a major disaster or a small dilemma, we can be at your home, building, or office to restore your loss as quickly as possible--most times within the hour after notification. We have the resources to handle any job--large or small. We will work with you and your insurance provider to mitigate the problem and get your life (or business) back to normal as soon as possible.

PCM specializes in property damage restoration, by providing 24/7/365 emergency services and reconstruction for residential and commercial properties. Our Mission is to provide victims of disaster with immediate restoration service with the utmost integrity, dedication to quality, and outstanding customer service.

With PCM as part of your company's solution to recover from natural and unpredictable disasters, you can take comfort in knowing that our quality craftsmanship and experienced personnel will serve all of your property restoration needs.
